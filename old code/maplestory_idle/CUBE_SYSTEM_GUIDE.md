# EQUIPMENT POTENTIAL CUBE SYSTEM

## Last Updated: December 2025

---

## OVERVIEW

The potential system adds bonus stats to equipment through 3 potential slots. There are two types:
- **Regular Potential** - Primary potential lines
- **Bonus Potential** - Secondary potential lines (separate system, same mechanics)

Each potential slot can roll stats from different tiers, with a separate pity system guaranteeing tier-ups after a certain number of cubes.

---

## CUBE TYPES

### Regular Potential Cube
- Rerolls all 3 Regular Potential lines
- Chance to tier up the potential
- Has its own pity counter

### Bonus Potential Cube  
- Rerolls all 3 Bonus Potential lines
- Chance to tier up the bonus potential
- Has its own (higher) pity counter

**Important:** You cannot mix and match - all 3 lines reroll together, and you must accept ALL new lines or keep ALL old lines.

## VALUABLE VS USELESS STATS

### Tier S (Best) - Worth Cubing For
- **Damage %** - Direct damage multiplier
- **Main Stat %** - DEX%/STR%/INT%/LUK% depending on class
- **Critical Damage %** (Special on Gloves)
- **Final Damage %** (Special on Bottom/Cape)
- **Defense Penetration %** (Special on Shoulder)
- **All Skills Level** (Special on Ring/Necklace)

### Tier A (Good)
- **Max Damage Multiplier** - Useful damage stat
- **Min Damage Multiplier** - Stabilizes damage
- **Critical Rate** - If under cap

### Tier B (Situational)
- **Attack Speed** - Only if you need it
- **Skill Cooldown** - Depends on build

### Tier F (Useless)
- **Defense %** - Minimal value
- **Max HP %** - Minimal value
- **Max MP %** - Worthless
- **Flat Main Stat** - Much worse than %
- **Wrong Main Stat** (e.g., INT% on Bowmaster)

## OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES

### Priority Order for Cubing

1. **Fix Bad Slot 1 Lines First**
   - Wrong main stat (e.g., LUK% on Bowmaster)
   - Completely useless stat (Defense%, Max MP%)
   - Cost: Low (just need any improvement)

2. **Target Good Stats on Slot 1**
   - Aim for Damage % or Main Stat %
   - Accept "good enough" (any damage stat)
   - Cost: Medium (~25-50 cubes)

3. **Chase Special Potentials**
   - Only on priority slots (Gloves, Ring, Necklace)
   - Budget 100+ cubes
   - Cost: High (100+ cubes, may take much more)

4. **Tier Up to Legendary**
   - Only after Slot 1 is acceptable
   - Budget for pity (333 cubes regular, 417 bonus)
   - Cost: Very High

5. **Tier Up to Mystic**
   - Whale territory
   - 714 cubes guaranteed
   - Cost: Extremely High (400k+ üíé)

### When to Stop Cubing

- **Stop immediately** if you hit a special potential
- **Stop** when Slot 1 has good stat at current tier
- **Stop** when you've spent your budget
- **Don't chase** perfect Slots 2/3 (76%/92% grey anyway)

### Budget Recommendations

| Goal | Budget | Expected Outcome |
|------|--------|------------------|
| Fix trash Slot 1 | 10-20 cubes | ~80% chance improvement |
| Get good Slot 1 stat | 50 cubes | ~85% chance of Damage% or Main% |
| Hunt special on Slot 1 | 100 cubes | ~63% chance to see special |
| Tier to Legendary | 333 cubes | 100% guaranteed (pity) |

## KEY FORMULAS

### Probability to NOT hit in N cubes
```
P(no hit) = (1 - p)^N
```

### Probability to hit AT LEAST ONCE in N cubes
```
P(at least one) = 1 - (1 - p)^N
```

### Expected cubes to hit (natural)
```
Expected = 1 / p
```

### Expected cubes to hit (with pity)
```
If pity < natural_expected:
    Expected = pity
Else:
    Expected ‚âà natural_expected √ó 0.9  (pity acts as safety net)
```
